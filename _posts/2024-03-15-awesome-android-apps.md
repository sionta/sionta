> [Acode](https://github.com/deadlyjack/Acode){: target="_blank}
is powerful text/code editor for android.

> [AdAway](https://github.com/AdAway/AdAway){: target="_blank"}
is a free and open source ad blocker for Android.

> [App Manager](https://github.com/MuntashirAkon/AppManager){: target="_blank"}
a full-featured package manager and viewer for Android.

> [Authy](https://authy.com/download/){: target="_blank"}
brings the future of strong authentication to the convenience of your Android device.

> [Bitwarden](https://github.com/bitwarden/clients){: target="_blank"}
is a secure password management application that helps you keep your passwords safe and secure.

> [Brave](https://github.com/brave/brave-browser){: target="_blank"}
is a privacy-focused browser, which automatically blocks most advertisements and website trackers in its default settings.

> [Bstation](){: target="_blank"}
the leading anime, comic and game (ACG) community in Southeast Asia as a place to create, watch and share interesting videos.

> [GCam](https://www.celsoazevedo.com/files/android/google-camera/){: target="_blank"}
a Google Camera Port, also known as Pixel Camera, is a modified version of the stock camera app shipped on Nexus and Pixel phones by Google.

> [Instander](https://thedise.me/instander/){: target="_blank"}
is Beautiful & Powerful Instagram Mod

> [MiXplorer](https://forum.xda-developers.com/t/app-2-2-mixplorer-v6-x-released-fully-featured-file-manager.1523691/){: target="_blank"}
is a fully-featured file manager for Android. It’s a versatile and powerful tool that allows you to manage files and folders on your device

> [NewPipe](https://github.com/TeamNewPipe/NewPipe/){: target="_blank"}
a libre lightweight streaming front-end for Android.

> [OpenVPN Connect](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.openvpn.openvpn){: target="_blank"}
is a virtual private network (VPN) system that implements techniques to create secure point-to-point or site-to-site connections in routed or bridged configurations and remote access facilities.

> [ProtonVPN](https://github.com/ProtonVPN/android-app){: target="_blank"}
is a VPN service that does not track or record your internet activity, and allows you to access Onion sites through the Tor network.

> [SD Maid](https://sdmaid.darken.eu/){: target="_blank"}
is an app that helps you manage files and apps on your Android device.

> [SD Maid 2/SE](https://github.com/d4rken-org/sdmaid-se){: target="_blank"}
is a trusted helper for your Android, to keep it clean & tidy

> [SuperFreezZ](https://gitlab.com/SuperFreezZ/SuperFreezZ){: target="_blank"}
is not yet another task manager promising to delete 10 GB of data per month or making your device 2x as fast.

> [Swift Backup](https://swiftapps.org/){: target="_blank"}
can back up your important data in minutes! It's fast, efficient, and offers an elegant design for a refreshing backup experience.

> [Termux](https://github.com/termux/termux-app){: target="_blank"}
is a free and open-source terminal emulator for Android which allows for running a Linux environment on an Android device.

> [YMusic](https://ymusic.io/){: target="_blank"}
is a web browser designed for Android devices that enhances the browsing experience on YouTube, such as reducing data consumption, play audio in background and more.
